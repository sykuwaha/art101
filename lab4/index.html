Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

The challenge of this lab was to pseudocode an everyday task and a game, and then turn them into JS comments.

Problems

The most difficult part of this lab was figuring out how to break down the tasks into subtasks. It was difficult to organize which was which, so we made several revisions to our pseudocode to organize it in a way that made sense.
